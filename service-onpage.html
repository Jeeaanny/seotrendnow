On-Page Website SEO

Optimizing every element of your web pages so Google fully understands what you do, who you serve, and why you deserve to rank — turning existing traffic into real leads.

Get a Free Audit

What's Included

On-page SEO is the art of making your website as clear and relevant as possible to both search engines and real visitors. I audit and optimize every page element — from title tags to internal links — to maximize your ranking potential without rebuilding anything from scratch.

  • Title tag & meta description optimization
  • Header structure (H1–H4) refinement
  • Keyword placement & density optimization
  • Internal linking architecture
  • Image optimization, compression & alt text
  • Schema markup & structured data implementation
  • URL structure improvement
  • Page speed enhancements

Who Is This For?

Any business with an existing website that wants to improve rankings without starting over. Especially valuable after a site redesign, content refresh, or if you have pages stuck on page 2 that just need a push.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will you change my website design?

No — on-page SEO focuses on content and code-level optimizations, not visual design. I work within your existing site.

How many pages do you optimize?

Scope depends on your package. Most engagements start with your highest-priority pages (homepage, service pages, location pages).

Do you need CMS access?

For implementation, yes. I work with WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, and most common platforms.
